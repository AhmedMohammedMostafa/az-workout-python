**Day 1: Chest and Triceps**

* Bench press: 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions
* Incline dumbbell press: 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions
* Flyes: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Triceps extensions: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Overhead triceps extensions: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions

**Day 2: Back and Biceps**

* Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions
* Lat pulldowns: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Seated rows: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions
* Bicep curls: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Hammer curls: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions

**Day 3: Rest**

**Day 4: Legs (Quadriceps)**

* Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions
* Leg press: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Leg extensions: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions
* Calf raises: 3 sets of 15-20 repetitions

**Day 5: Legs (Hamstrings)**

* Hamstring curls: 3 sets of 10-15 repetitions
* Leg curls: 3 sets of 12-15 repetitions
* Good mornings: 3 sets of 8-12 repetitions
* Calf raises: 3 sets of 15-20 repetitions

**Additional Notes:**

* Warm up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio before each workout.
* Use weights that are challenging but allow you to maintain good form.
* Rest for 1-2 minutes between sets.
* Aim for 10-12 repetitions per set for hypertrophy (muscle growth).
* Prioritize compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups simultaneously.
* Include 2-3 sets of ab exercises at the end of each workout.
* Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
* Supplement with protein powder if desired to support muscle recovery.
* Consider consulting with a registered dietitian to optimize your nutrition for weight loss goals.
